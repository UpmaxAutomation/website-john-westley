### Headline

### The Problem
Most B2B revenue problems get diagnosed wrong.

The pipeline stalls. The VP Sales is blamed. A new methodology is tried. Six months later, the same problem.

Because the problem was never the people. It was the motion.

There are six structural patterns that silently tax every dollar of B2B revenue — broken handoffs, missing retention, invisible growth ceilings, uncollected expansion, conversion drag, misaligned teams. Most companies have two or three of them active and don't know which ones.

Structural problems don't respond to execution pressure. They just keep running.

### What John Does
Revenue journey diagnostics and redesign for B2B companies at $3M–$50M.

**Phase 1 — Revenue Journey Audit** (Weeks 1–3)
Full diagnostic across every stage of the revenue journey. Identifies which structural patterns are active and where revenue is leaking.

**Phase 2 — Structural Diagnosis** (Weeks 3–5)
Root-cause analysis. Prioritized, sequenced intervention plan. Board-ready deliverables.

**Phase 3 — Redesign & Rebuild** (Weeks 5–16)
Implementation. New motion designed and operational. John stays through until it holds.

### Why John
He's held every seat: CRO, VP Sales, VP Marketing, CEO.

He diagnoses before he prescribes — because the right fix depends on what's actually broken.

He stays through implementation — because a diagnosis in a slide deck doesn't fix anything.

### Results

- **$3.5M recovered in 6 months** — single structural fix (expansion gap)

### The First Step
